Home Remedies


Most home remedies are simple and easy, such as using heat pads, herbal teas, and gentle exercises to alleviate symptoms. Some of my favorite ways to regain balance are listed below.

Heat Pads

Applying heat to the affected areas can help soothe discomfort. Though many suggest heated blankets, ranging around $40+, there are also more affordable options such as disposable heat patches that come around a dozen per box.
